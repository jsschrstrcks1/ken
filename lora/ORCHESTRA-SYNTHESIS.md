*Source: Perplexity (OpenAI 2026 pricing data) / GPT validation*

### 2. **Register Smearing Will Break Persona** 🎭 HIGH RISK

- **The problem:** Mixing 11 disparate voice corpora (sermons + sheep logs + commit messages + grandmother recipes) in a single LoRA causes stylistic dilution
- **Evidence:** Standard 2026 practices use per-domain adapters or extreme weighting to maintain persona
- **Impact:** Single LoRA will fail to preserve "Careful Not Clever" tone across contexts
- **Phased solves this:** v1 locks in core epistemic integrity (Layer 1) with proven voice, then v2 can layer voice adapters separately

*Source: Perplexity (primary), confirmed by GPT*

### 3. **Epistemic Integrity Must Come First** ⚖️ STRUCTURAL

- **Layer 1 (Epistemic Foundation)** is the foundation for Layers 2-3
- **Phased approach:** Deploy v1.1 as "Stabilized Epistemic Layer" with Behavioral Backbone (Layer 2 core)
- **Then:** Add Voice Corpora + Reasoning Traces as secondary stylistic LoRA after Layer 1 is validated
- **Compliance win:** Satisfies 2026 FTC "clear and conspicuous" disclosure mandates by correctly labeling internal state (verified/unverified) before adding complexity

*Source: Perplexity (regulatory framing) / GPT (structural integrity)*
