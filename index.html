Who is Ken Baker?

Ken is a Florida-based follower of King Jesus—a shepherd of both sheep and souls. He holds a Bachelor of Arts in Biblical Studies from Trinity College of Florida and is nearing completion of his Master of Divinity at Southeastern Baptist Theological Seminary in Wake Forest, North Carolina. He delights in traveling to witness firsthand the beauty of God’s creation and in capturing those moments through photography to share with the world. In every sunrise and storm, he sees a glimpse of divine artistry. Ken tends a small flock of actual sheep and pastors a small church in Florida devoted to one simple mission: to take strangers and make them family through the glorious gospel of Jesus Christ.

Frequently Asked Questions

What projects does Ken Baker work on?

Ken Baker maintains three main projects: Flickers of Majesty (artistic photography capturing divine light), Cruising in the Wake (cruise planning resources and ship reviews), and Lakeview Fellowship Church (a welcoming Florida church community in Hudson).

What is Flickers of Majesty?

Flickers of Majesty is an artistic photography project featuring evocative glimpses of majesty where light dances upon the canvas of creation, inviting contemplation of the divine in everyday wonders.

What is Cruising in the Wake?

Cruising in the Wake provides curated cruise planning resources, ship reviews, dining tips, port guides, and accessibility advice for effortless seafaring adventures. It offers practical information for cruise travelers seeking smoother sailings.

What is Lakeview Fellowship Church?

Lakeview Fellowship Church is a welcoming Florida church community in Hudson where we seek to take strangers and make family through worship, events, and heartfelt service.

He holds a Bachelor of Arts in Biblical Studies from Trinity College of Florida and is completing his Master of Divinity at Southeastern Baptist Theological Seminary.
